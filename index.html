The Roots

The roots is a West African story about slavery.
The main character; Kunta Kinte is born, in a small village during a time when Americans were buying slaves from the Coast of West Africa.
He and some of his kinsmen are captured after being betrayed by men from a neighbouring village.
He does not want to forget his roots and therefore tries severally to escape enslavement.
He even rejects the European sounding name given to him by his masters wife (Toby).
However, after a long time, he resigns to his fate.
The movie continues by narrating the life of his offspring long after his death.
